Your Wellness package includes 2 trips for 5 days with
a 21-day break in between. You will enjoy hospitality
and leisure opportunities in Dubai during your stay and
get vaccinated against Covid-19 during each of the trips.

1st trip for 5 days / 1st vaccination

Travel to Dubai for 5 days. The first vaccination is administered on a dedicated day and time pre-booked for you. You will enjoy your stay in Dubai and leave the UAE in 5 days to come back for your second pre-booked trip in at least 21 days.

2nd trip for 5 days / 2nd vaccination

The second vaccination is done no earlier then in 21 days after the first dose. Your second prebooked trip will last for another 5 days. The second vaccination is administered on a dedicated day and time, pre-booked for you. Upon the completion of the vaccination programme, you can travel across the UAE.

Sightseeing

The following excursions are included in your Wellness Travel Package. Find out about other travel & leisure opportunities
in Dubai at visitdubai.com and let us know if you would like
to explore other sightseeing options.

Dubai Half Day
City Tour

Tour the best of Dubai's beautiful landmarks, including the magnificent Jumeirah Mosque built in 1979. Pass by Jumeriah Beach and Burj Al Arab, and finish with a visit to the fascinating Palm Jumeirah with a short stop in Atlantis.

Dubai Creek
Sightseeing Tour

Take a step back in time with the Creek sightseeing tour. The Creek is a historic and fascinating part of Dubai, which in the past marked the divide between the Deira and Bur Dubai neighbourhoods of the city. The land alongside the Bur Dubai Creek is also where the Baniyas settlers first lived in the original days of Dubai.

Desert Safari —
Dinner Included
