Medical criteria for eligibility for vaccination

To be administered the COVID-19 Sinopharma or equivalent vaccine, the Customer must
not have any of the below:
  1. History of severe COVID 19 infection needed oxygen during admission.
  2. Lactating women with a baby less than 6 months old.
  3. Pregnant women (positive urine test at the same day) or planning to be pregnant within 3 months.
  4. History of sever reaction associated with vaccine e g, anaphylaxis
    1. Bleeding disorders: Severe hemophilia A and B, Glanzmann Thrombasthenia, Von Willbrand Disease VWB) Type 3
    2. Live attenuated vaccine within 30 days
    3. Killed vaccine within 14 days
    4. Recent history of Guillain Barre Syndrome GBS) or transverse myelitis in the past 12 months
  5. Active cancer or in remission for less than 6 months.
  6. Chronic diseases with acute symptoms. Such as acute symptoms
    for Immunocompromised (auto immune diseases, Immunosuppressive therapy, HIV,
    and posttransplant), Renal failure, Diabetes Miletus, Coronary Artery Diseases including HTN, Liver diseases, Lung diseases including Asthma, COPD, Bleeding disorders,
    and Central Nervous System including CVA, MS and Epilepsy.
During the 2nd dose of the vaccination to proceed, individual should
not have:
  1. Female urine pregnancy positive result
  2. Patient with high fever (axillary temperature ≥ 39.0° C) lasting for 3 days after
    the previous dose of vaccine.
  3. Serious adverse reactions related to previous dose of vaccine
